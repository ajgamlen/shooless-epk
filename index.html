After making waves throughout the Tamaki Makaurau music scene with their latest singles, 'Ignorant Bliss', 'Lonely Playground', and 'Sunsets', SHOOLESS finally lift the lid on their debut album, Lonely Playground.



Lonely Playground
(Debut Album)
  • Order Vinyl

Their record spans visceral alternative rock right through to uplifting indie groove. Each track promises to unravel a narrative of personal struggle, self-reflection, and eventual resolution.

"Very few artists I have seen perform break the fourth wall between artist and audience in the way Shooless does."

- Hannah Nott, Ponsonby News

"Their essence and echoes of PJ Harvey, Jeff Buckley and Tool are boiled in the voodoo of King Krule."

"Different genre swords from jazz fusion and trip hop cut the ephemeral idea into perfected audio cathedrals."

- Floyd Lee, The 13th Floor
